About me

Hi! ​I'm Tobias, a self taught artist and programmer student from Argentina, I started playing when I was 5 years old, and since then I knew that I wanted to be part of the world of videogame creation.

Here you can see some of the projects i've had the opportunity to contributing to, and som​e of the personal works i've been working on. I have a strong perseverance for learning new things and taking on challenges, and i always like to put some extra time in polishing my creations, to make sure the creative vision is achieved in the best possible way. I've always been extremely curious in the artistical and technical side of games, the way they are created, from the very bottom of the system to the marketing side, this proactive curiosity lead me to were i'm today!

If you have any questions please feel free to contact me :)
