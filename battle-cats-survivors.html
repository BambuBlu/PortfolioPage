Wath is BattleCats Survivors?

BattleCats Survivors immerses you in an arcade shooter roguelite experience, placing you in command of a formidable cat squadron that instinctively strikes down nearby adversaries. Strategically combine an array of heroes to unlock exclusive class bonuses, crafting unparalleled character synergies, and expertly navigate your unstoppable party as they relentlessly obliterate endless waves of foes.

Design process summary

We set out to create a cute and magical world by adding a combination of the genres: Roguelite, Snake, Bullet Hell, all mixed with a cute cat-like design. BattleCats Survivors is built from the ground up in Godot. We spent the first few months of the project creating a solid conceptual foundation to work on. Then we start adding new systems, designs and features.

Some of the  game systems and features included in the game are:

  • Cats automatically engage and attack nearby enemies.
  • Merge Cats of the same classes to access special class bonuses.
  • Earning gold from the battle victories lets you buy more and new cats in the slot machine.
  • Strengthen the cats by acquiring multiple copies of the same character, enhancing their abilities.

...and a bunch more. We also had made many, many ideas for new systems to be incorporated in to the game. Like a endless surviving battle, many new games+, a PvP type of arena, ​design new heroes and enemys that can be unlock by making different types of runs, etc...

Even with a "somewhat small" project like this, it was very important to me to create a game that could be played many times without getting bored of the aesthetics or mechanics and that could be played by anyone, from children to adults and at any time of the day, from returning home to in a cafe.

One shortcoming that I could highlight is that we were missing a good world creation that would give much more context to the game and could help derive much more content from it. The good thing? It's something we're working on!
