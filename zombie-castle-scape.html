ZombieCastle Scape

Status: in Development

Platforms: Mobile

Studio: Lightvessel

Team size: ​2

Project lenght:  3 months (in progress)

Role/responsibilities: Game artist and designer

Engine and tools: Godot, Illustrator, Adobe Xd, Photoshop, Adobe Audition

ZombieCastle Scape is a cute Hardcore platformer where you play as a soldier who turns into a zombie thanks to an evil necromancer. In order to escape you will need to jump, dodge and run your way through numerous ever-changing levels that get harder each time you succeed. You’ll need to try, die into pieces, and try again if you want to conquer and become human again!

Details about my contributions on this project will be updated at a later date.
